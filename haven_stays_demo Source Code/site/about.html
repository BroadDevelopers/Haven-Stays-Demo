Our Story

Stay somewhere worth remembering.

Haven Stays began with a simple frustration: the most beautiful places to stay were the hardest to find, and the easiest to find were never worth remembering.

How we started

Two travelers, one stubborn belief

In 2021, frequent travelers Elena Marchetti and Tomas Bergquist were tired of vacation rentals that looked nothing like their photos. They'd spent years finding the hidden gems — the architect's cabin, the fisherman's cottage, the designer's pied-à-terre — and decided to build a collection that held the same standard.

Five years later, Haven Stays is still small, still obsessive, and still personally vetted by the same team that started it — now backed by local hosts and design obsessives across 32 destinations.

What we believe

Three principles we won't compromise

🏠

Personally vetted

Every home is visited by our team before it joins the collection. We check the views, the beds, the Wi-Fi, and the coffee maker.

✨

Design-forward

We choose homes with character — designed by architects, styled by people with taste. No beige hotel suites.

🤝

Concierge-first

From booking to check-out, our AI concierge and human specialists are here 24/7. You're never on your own.
