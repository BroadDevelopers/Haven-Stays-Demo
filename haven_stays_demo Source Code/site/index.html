Questions?

Everything you need to know

How do I book a Haven Stays home?+

Browse our curated collection, check availability for your dates, and reserve with our concierge. A Haven Stays specialist confirms your booking and sends a secure payment link within one hour.

What's included in my stay?+

Every Haven Stays home includes premium linens, a fully equipped kitchen, high-speed Wi-Fi, and 24/7 concierge support. Many homes feature private pools, hot tubs, and dedicated workspaces.

Is there a loyalty program?+

Yes — Haven Stays loyalty members save an extra 5% on every booking. Stays of 7+ nights automatically receive a 10% weekly discount, stackable with loyalty savings.

Can I talk to a concierge before booking?+

Absolutely. Our AI concierge, Haven, is available 24/7 in the chat widget — it can search properties, check availability, build a custom quote with savings, and place a hold on your favorite home.
